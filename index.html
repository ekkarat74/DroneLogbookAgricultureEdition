Agricultural drone operations require accurate mission planning, consistent spray documentation, reliable maintenance tracking and precise field-level reporting. DroneLogbook Ag Edition delivers:
  • Field-ready mission planning tools for designing crop-spray routes, assigning applicators and managing field boundaries
  • Real-time flight logging and telemetry import, supporting 80+ UAV formats used in agricultural aircraft
  • Integrated spray logs for documenting application rates, tank loads, boundaries, prescriptions & operator notes
  • FAA compliance workflows supporting Part 137, Part 107, Part 108 and state agricultural aviation requirements
  • Fleet & inventory management for drones, spray systems, payloads, tanks, and batteries
  • Maintenance dashboards to track inspections, pump cycles, spray-system lifespan and airframe service intervals
  • Digital forms for field authorizations, grower requests and safety documentation
