Built for connected, compliant, and scalable drone operations

DroneLogbook is an industry leader in developing tools and flight data importers that streamline drone operations from the field to the back office. Our platform is designed for organizations that need reliable, audit-ready records, powerful integrations, and the flexibility to grow—whether you’re running a small fleet or a global drone program.

Our Private Label platform extends this capability to large enterprises that need their own branded, centrally managed environment. It gives operations, safety, and compliance teams a powerful control center for standardizing workflows, enforcing rules, and supervising activity across multiple business units and regions.

We also support organizations operating globally, providing solutions tailored to local regulatory requirements and market needs so your drone program stays compliant wherever you fly.

Integrations – Connect Your Entire Drone Tech Stack
DroneLogbook is built around integrations. Our platform accepts telemetry files from over 50 hardware and software sources, so you can bring all your flight data into a single, structured system without changing how your pilots like to fly.

We support leading drone manufacturers and autopilot systems such as DJI, Parrot, Wingtra, senseFly, Autel, Yuneec, XAG, Wingcopter, Vantage Robotics, and more, plus popular ground control and mission-planning tools like UGCS, DroneHarmony, Sky-Drones, Simnet, Zephyr, DroneLink, Flytbase, Q Ground Control, Mission Planner, and others.

All these data sources are normalized into a consistent format, allowing you to:
  • • Centralize flight history, assets, maintenance, and compliance records
  • • Reduce manual data entry and spreadsheet chaos
  • • Run consistent reporting across mixed fleets and tools
  • • Feed clean, structured data into your analytics, BI, or AI workflows

DroneLogbook Mobile Apps – Innovation in the Field

DroneLogbook is built on a secure cloud infrastructure with a modern web application and dedicated mobile apps, giving your team full access to critical data whether they’re in the office or in the field. The mobile app supports offline use and syncs seamlessly with your cloud account, so flights recorded in low-coverage areas are safely uploaded when connectivity returns.

In the field, pilots can view up-to-date airspace status, local weather, and solar activity indices that may impact flight safety, helping them make better go/no-go decisions before takeoff.

Our additional Android application, DLBSync, simplifies importing flights from major mobile drone flight control apps directly into DroneLogbook. It can collect flights while offline or in poor coverage and then upload them automatically once Wi-Fi or mobile data is available—eliminating cables, manual exports, and lost logs.

Together, these innovations ensure that DroneLogbook isn’t just a place to store data—it’s the connected backbone of your drone operations, from integrations and private-label deployments to global compliance and field-ready mobile tools.
