DroneLogbook Advantage

DroneLogbook delivers an end-to-end drone operations and compliance platform for UAV programs of any size. From commercial enterprises to public safety teams and training organizations, we automate the record-keeping, reporting, and oversight you need to stay safe, efficient, and audit-ready.

Fleet Management
DroneLogbook gives you a complete fleet management workspace for every aircraft and component you operate. Build inspection and maintenance plans, log unscheduled events, and connect follow-up tasks so nothing slips through the cracks. Inspection and maintenance records are linked, letting you update schedules automatically whenever work is performed.

Every flight you log—especially via automated log imports—updates usage, inspections, and maintenance in real time. That means your team always sees the latest status for each drone, battery, and critical component without extra data entry.

    Main Features

    Log your flights with detailed information

    Import telemetry logs to automatically populate flight records, flight paths, and 3D replays. DroneLogbook supports many log formats and major drone manufacturers so your team can log more and type less.

    Generate custom and legal reports

    Create branded PDF reports with your organization’s identity and required regulatory fields. Generate compliance-ready reports for aviation authorities in seconds so you can quickly respond to audits or client requests.

    Store all organization & flight documents

    Centralize mission-critical documentation—pre-flight forms, checklists, risk assessments, waivers, SOPs, and more. Build custom forms and workflows so your documentation process mirrors how your operation really works.

    Manage drone inspections and maintenance

    Configure inspection and maintenance plans for every aircraft and asset. DroneLogbook notifies you as inspections or service dates approach so your fleet remains airworthy and ready for deployment.

    Plan missions

    Plan missions from a single dashboard: review airspace, attach documentation, and route missions for approval. Risk assessments, checklists, and mission files live alongside your flights for a complete operational view.

    Track field-level chemical applications

    Track every chemical application at the field level and maintain a complete digital history for each block, farm, or polygon. See at a glance what was applied, at what rate, and under which conditions so you can answer compliance, agronomy, and customer questions in seconds.
    Each application record can include:
    • • Field or polygon name and boundaries
    • • Products, tank mix, rate, and application method
    • • Weather at time of spray (wind, temperature, humidity, and more)
    • • Aircraft and spray hardware used (drone, spray system, payload, supporting equipment)
    • • Pilot, VO, and ground crew involved in the operation
    • • Flight path, duration, and coverage
    Over time, this builds a searchable field history accessible anytime, from any device. Confirm label compliance and REI/PHI timing, verify what was done for each grower or customer, and export field-level reports for growers, agronomists, and regulators in just a few clicks.

    Manage your organization and pilots

    Track pilot currency, qualifications, and renewals in one system. Store pilot and organization documents, assign roles, and define skills and capabilities (by regulation, operation type, business case, and tools) with the option to add custom skill profiles.

    Manage your equipment

    Monitor usage and service history for payloads, sensors, and ground equipment. Understand actual operating time for each device and align maintenance with real-world utilization.

    Manage battery cycles

    Record battery charges and track performance over time to identify aging or under-performing packs. Use cycle history and health trends to keep unsafe batteries out of the field and extend the life of good ones.

    Report your flight incidents

    Log incidents with linked pilots, drones, and equipment, plus supporting documentation. Generate incident reports for your compliance or safety team and maintain a clear record of what happened and how it was resolved.
