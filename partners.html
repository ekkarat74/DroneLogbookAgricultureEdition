Ag Drone Industry Integration & Partnerships

DroneLogbook Ag is designed to be the data backbone for agricultural drone operations. Instead of locking you into a single ecosystem, we’re focused on working with any spray drone hardware or ag software supplier to streamline workflows, reduce double entry, and improve ROI on every drone, tank, and acre.

Our platform connects flight logs and application data with field boundaries, crops, products, weather, pilots, and hardware so ag service providers, co-ops, and growers can see exactly what was applied, where, and when—no matter which drone or flight app was used. Through APIs, log-import tools, and web services, partners can push data directly into DroneLogbook Ag and turn raw telemetry into clean, useful records for operations and compliance.

DJI Agras Support Today – More Ag Integrations Coming

Right now, DroneLogbook Ag includes dedicated support for DJI Agras spray drones. You can:
  • • Import DJI Agras flight and application logs
  • • Auto-populate field-level chemical application records
  • • Link each mission to pilots, equipment, tank mixes, and weather
  • • Build a complete history for every field or paddock

This means operators can already use DroneLogbook Ag as a precision agriculture record-keeping system for drone spraying—tracking application rate, coverage, and conditions for each job.

At the same time, we’re actively working with additional drone and ag-tech companies to automate the drone application industry end-to-end. Our goal is to let you mix different spray drones, mapping tools, and farm-management platforms while still maintaining one trusted system of record for:

  • • Field-level chemical application and fertiliser history
  • • Products, rates, and tank mixes
  • • Wind, temperature, and other weather conditions at time of application
  • • Aircraft, payload, and pilot information for every mission

Why Partner With DroneLogbook Ag?

Whether you build spray drones, flight-control software, agronomy tools, or farm-management platforms, partnering with DroneLogbook Ag helps you deliver more value to your customers:
  • • Hardware & OEM partners – Ship your drones with a ready-made ag operations and compliance platform. Give customers automatic logging and chemical tracking at the field level from day one.
  • • Software & agronomy partners – Sync maps, prescriptions, and field data via API. Eliminate manual data entry and give agronomists and growers a clearer picture of what was applied and how fields performed.
  • • Service providers & co-ops – Use integrated tools to prove work performed, simplify billing, and deliver professional application reports back to growers, complete with field maps, products, and weather data

By connecting your solution to DroneLogbook Ag, you help ag operators:

  • • Save time on paperwork and manual log uploads
  • • Improve accuracy of compliance and chemical-use records
  • • Increase utilisation and ROI of spray drones and software tools

DroneLogbook Ag Mobile & Field Tools

DroneLogbook Ag runs on secure cloud infrastructure with a modern web app and mobile tools built for field work. Pilots and ground crews can:
  • • Work online or offline and sync when back in coverage
  • • Review fields, missions, and recent applications on a phone or tablet
  • • Capture checklists, notes, and forms at the edge of the paddock

Our Android companion app, DLBSync, helps move flights from supported mobile flight-control apps straight into DroneLogbook Ag—no cables, no manual exports. This keeps your spray records complete and accurate, supporting better agronomy decisions, smoother audits, and higher ROI from every drone application season.

We support more than 80 flight log formats!

DroneLogbook works by importing flight logs via automated or manual telemetry importation. Import flight logs to auto-fill flight data, view GPS trace, and play back in 3D. We support log files from all major manufacturers: DJI, Parrot, Autel, Wingcopter, FlyAbility, Wingtra, SenseFly, Yuneec... More than 80 log types can be imported.

DroneLogbook Mobile Apps

The DroneLogbook solution is based on a cloud infrastructure with secure data storage, online web application accompanied by our mobile application for offline access and easy synchronization with your cloud-based account. In addition, the mobile app provides you with access to the latest airspace status updates, local weather and solar index reading that might affect your operations.


Another Android mobile application, DLBSync, simplifies import of your flights from major mobile drone flight control apps natively to your DroneLogbook account. This app can sync flights into DLB Sync from your flight control apps when offline or in poor mobile coverage, then upload flights to DroneLogbook account when you have mobile or Wi-Fi coverage.
