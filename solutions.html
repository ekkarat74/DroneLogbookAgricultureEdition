DroneLogbook Solutions

DroneLogbook is actively working to provide industry specific solutions to help companies better manage their drone operations. We recognize that different industries have different ways of operating and that we as a drone industry software solutions provider need to provide different solutions to address the differing needs of education, public safety, energy, construction, general commercial and others. As such we have created specially tailored platforms to fit the needs of these industry sectors.

DroneLogbook also offers a Private Label version (White Label) of our platform for those with more complex and/or larger scale drone operations. The platform is modular and configurable with a detailed administrative panel providing custom notifications and detailed operational oversight.

DroneLogbook For Commercial
DroneLogbook was created to help commercial UAV operators plan, manage and track their drone flight operations to help maintain regulatory compliance and better manage their business. Our platform provides all the tools needed to support the commercial drone operations. As the industry grows we continue to add new features and integrate with partners to provide a seamless end to end fleet management and compliance solution.

DroneLogbook for Education
If you are a drone training or educational institution with the need for a plan that allows you to rotate students on and off quickly, then contact us about our education vertical. These plans are a version of our private label platform designed to allow you to bring on students, train them, track their flights and then rotate them off all within a fixed budget. This program is great solution for public and private drone / rpas/ uav training courses, from four year colleges to union retraining programs.

DroneLogbook for Agriculture
www.dronelogbook.ag
DLB Ag Edition was developed with the FAA Part 137 UAV operator in mind and blends the power of DLB with the needs of the agricultural custom applicator. Private Label (White Label) Version
The DroneLogbook Private Label (White Label) platform is modular and configurable, dedicated cloud instance of our public drone operations compliance and drone fleet management software.
The key features of this platform vs the public Dronelogbook.com site:

Data Structure
  • Customizable data tables (Flight Types, Equipment, Drone Components)
  • Custom Forms and Templates:
    • Drone Inspection
    • Mission Plan
    • Custom Reports (Access All Data Tables)
    • Custom Documents – Checklists, Form Builder, Risk Assessment

Operations
  • Ability to Silo users into separate organizations with master oversight. Can also share information between organizations if needed
  • Mission request: ability to have 3rd parties outline flight areas (while keeping your data hidden)
  • Ability to auto notify if events are triggered (flight, operations, compliance, personnel). Things like flight altitude, overdue inspections, pilot currency, etc
  • Configurable platform allowing users to maintain their workflows


Administrative
  • Customer branding (colors/logo)
  • Private cloud storage infrastructure. On-demand specific region/country hosting available
  • Customizable (Custom programming available to modify platform for a fee)
  • Less expensive than our public server at scale


This platform is very attractive to those organizations with multiple offices/business lines that have a need to manage scaling operations. In addition, we offer a special version for Public Safety that is modified to accommodate their needs. Special pricing is available for Public Safety, Government and Educational organizations.

Sectors Currently Using Private Labels:
Education – Energy – Government – Mining – Media – Public Safety – Agriculture – Asset Owners (Transportation, Telco).

DroneLogbook Mobile Apps

The DroneLogbook solution is based on a cloud infrastructure with secure data storage, online web application accompanied by our mobile application for offline access and easy synchronization with your cloud based account. In addition, the mobile app provides you with access to the latest airspace status updates, local weather and solar index reading that might affect your operations.


Another Android mobile application, DLBSync, simplifies import of your flights from major mobile drone flight control apps natively to your DroneLogbook account. This app can sync flights into DLB Sync from your flight control apps when offline or in poor mobile coverage, then upload flights to DroneLogbook account when you have mobile or WIFI coverage.
